Federal Agencies and their Executives

Department

Top Executive(s)

Other Key Executives

Description of the Organization

Department of Agriculture (USDA)

Secretary Tom Vilsack

Deputy Secretary Dr. Jewel Bronaugh; Undersecretary for Rural Development Xochitl Torres Small

The USDA manages national policies related to agriculture, farming, food safety, and rural development. It works to ensure food security and promote sustainable agriculture.

Department of Commerce

Secretary Gina Raimondo

Deputy Secretary Don Graves; Undersecretary for International Trade Marisa Lago

The Department oversees economic growth, technological innovation, and international trade. It plays a key role in advancing U.S. competitiveness and supports American businesses.

Department of Defense (DoD)

Secretary Lloyd Austin

Deputy Secretary Kathleen Hicks; Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff Gen. Mark Milley

The DoD is responsible for national defense and military policies. It oversees the armed forces and coordinates U.S. defense strategy.

Department of Education

Secretary Miguel Cardona

Deputy Secretary Cindy Marten; Undersecretary James Kvaal

This department handles the federal government's role in education policy, supporting K-12 and higher education institutions, and advancing student equity.

Department of Energy

Secretary Jennifer Granholm

Deputy Secretary David Turk; Undersecretary for Science and Energy Geraldine Richmond

The DOE leads in ensuring U.S. energy security and advancing clean energy technology, managing nuclear energy and scientific research.

Department of Health and Human Services (HHS)

Secretary Xavier Becerra

Deputy Secretary Andrea Palm; Administrator of the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services Chiquita Brooks-LaSure

HHS oversees public health programs, including Medicare and Medicaid, and enacts policies to improve public health and health equity.

Department of Homeland Security (DHS)

Secretary Alejandro Mayorkas

Deputy Secretary John Tien; Administrator of FEMA Deanne Criswell

DHS focuses on national security, emergency response, and border protection. It plays a crucial role in preventing terrorism and managing immigration.

Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD)

Secretary Marcia Fudge

Deputy Secretary Adrianne Todman; Assistant Secretary for Policy Development and Research Anna Maria Farias

HUD addresses housing needs, urban development, and policies aimed at affordable housing and homelessness prevention.

Department of the Interior

Secretary Deb Haaland

Deputy Secretary Tommy Beaudreau; Director of the National Park Service Chuck Sams

The DOI manages national parks, wildlife conservation, and federal land policy, promoting sustainable use and environmental protection.

Department of Justice (DOJ)

Attorney General Merrick Garland

Deputy Attorney General Lisa Monaco; FBI Director Christopher Wray

DOJ enforces the law and ensures fair administration of justice in the U.S. It oversees federal law enforcement and civil rights protection.

Department of Labor

Acting Secretary Julie Su

Deputy Secretary Julie A. Su (pending confirmation); Assistant Secretary for Employment and Training Taryn Williams

The Department enforces labor laws, ensures fair wages, workplace safety, and addresses workers' rights in the private sector.

Department of State

Secretary Antony Blinken

Deputy Secretary Wendy Sherman; U.S. Ambassador to the UN Linda Thomas-Greenfield

The State Department handles foreign relations, diplomacy, and the protection of U.S. citizens abroad. It manages U.S. embassies and consulates worldwide.

Department of Transportation (DOT)

Secretary Pete Buttigieg

Deputy Secretary Polly Trottenberg; Administrator of FAA Billy Nolen

DOT oversees federal transportation policy, ensuring safe, efficient, and sustainable transportation systems, including roads, aviation, and rail.

Department of Treasury

Secretary Janet Yellen

Deputy Secretary Wally Adeyemo; Undersecretary for Domestic Finance Nellie Liang

The Treasury manages U.S. finances, including fiscal policy, tax collection, and financial services. It plays a key role in economic regulation.

Department of Veterans Affairs (VA)

Secretary Denis McDonough

Deputy Secretary Donald Remy; Undersecretary for Health Dr. Shereef Elnahal

VA provides comprehensive services to U.S. military veterans, including healthcare, pensions, and benefits. It ensures veterans' needs are met.

Environmental Protection Agency (EPA)

Administrator Michael Regan

Deputy Administrator Janet McCabe; Assistant Administrator for Air and Radiation Dr. Joe Goffman

The EPA enforces environmental protection laws, regulating pollutants and addressing climate change, and ensuring public health standards related to air and water quality.

Small Business Administration (SBA)

Administrator Isabella Casillas Guzman

Deputy Administrator Tayyib Smith; Associate Administrator for Entrepreneurial Development Jayne L. Simpson

The SBA supports small businesses through financial aid, educational resources, and regulatory guidance, promoting growth and job creation.

Office of Management and Budget (OMB)

Director Shalanda Young

Deputy Director for Management Jason Gray; Administrator of the Office of Information and Regulatory Affairs

The OMB coordinates the implementation of the President’s budget, oversees federal agency performance, and develops regulatory policy.

U.S. Trade Representative (USTR)

Ambassador Katherine Tai

Deputy U.S. Trade Representative Sarah Bianchi; Assistant U.S. Trade Representative for Southeast Asia, Barbara Weisel

USTR leads trade negotiations and implements U.S. trade policy, ensuring that global trade relationships align with national interests.
